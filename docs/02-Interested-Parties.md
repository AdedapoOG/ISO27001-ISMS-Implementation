# Interested Parties Register

## 1. Purpose
This document identifies relevant interested parties to the ISMS and their requirements in accordance with ISO/IEC 27001:2022.

---

## 2. Interested Parties and Requirements

| Interested Party | Requirement | Why It Matters |
|-----------------|------------|----------------|
| Customers | Protection of personal data, secure transactions | Maintains trust and ensures GDPR compliance |
| Employees | Secure access to systems, data protection | Enables safe and efficient operations |
| Regulators (e.g. ICO) | Compliance with GDPR and data protection laws | Avoids legal penalties and sanctions |
| Cloud Providers (AWS, Google) | Secure configuration and proper usage | Prevents misconfigurations and data breaches |
| Payment Processors | Secure handling of financial transactions | Ensures financial data protection |
| Business Owner | Business continuity and data protection | Protects revenue and reputation |

---

## 3. Legal and Regulatory Requirements

- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- UK Data Protection Act 2018
- ISO/IEC 27001:2022 standard requirements

---

## 4. Review
This register will be reviewed annually or when significant changes occur.
