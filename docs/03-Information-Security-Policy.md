# Information Security Policy

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this policy is to establish a framework for protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information within GenStudio Ltd.

---

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to all employees, systems, and data within the defined ISMS scope.

---

## 3. Objectives
- Protect customer and business data from unauthorized access
- Ensure availability of systems and services
- Maintain data integrity and accuracy
- Comply with legal, regulatory, and contractual requirements

---

## 4. Principles

### 4.1 Confidentiality
Information shall only be accessible to authorized individuals.

### 4.2 Integrity
Information must be accurate and protected from unauthorized modification.

### 4.3 Availability
Information and systems must be accessible when required.

---

## 5. Responsibilities

### Management
- Ensure implementation of the ISMS
- Provide resources for security controls

### Employees
- Follow security policies and procedures
- Report security incidents promptly

---

## 6. Compliance
All employees and stakeholders must comply with this policy. Violations may result in disciplinary action.

---

## 7. Review
This policy will be reviewed annually or when significant changes occur.
