# ISMS Scope Document

## 1. Purpose
This document defines the scope of the Information Security Management System (ISMS) for GenStudio Ltd in alignment with ISO/IEC 27001:2022.

---

## 2. Organisation Overview
GenStudio Ltd is a digital creative and e-commerce brand focused on AI-generated fashion content, social media automation, and online product sales.

---

## 3. Scope Boundaries

### In Scope:
- Social media automation systems (n8n workflows)
- Content creation platforms
- Cloud storage (Google Drive / AWS S3)
- E-commerce platforms (e.g., Shopify)
- Internal devices (laptops, mobile devices)

### Out of Scope:
- Third-party platforms not controlled by GenStudio (e.g., Instagram infrastructure)
- Customer devices

---

## 4. Locations
- Remote operations (UK-based)
- Cloud-hosted infrastructure

---

## 5. Information Assets Covered
- Customer data (names, emails, orders)
- Digital content (images, designs)
- Automation workflows
- Business credentials and access keys

---

## 6. Assumptions
- All systems rely heavily on cloud services
- Access is role-based where possible
- Data protection regulations (e.g., GDPR) apply

---

## 7. Dependencies
- Third-party cloud providers
- Payment processors
- Social media platforms

---

## 8. Review
This scope will be reviewed annually or upon significant business changes.
